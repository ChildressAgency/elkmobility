Handbook for nursing: "How many nurses does it take to get a patient up?"

This handbook is my uber-practical guide and gift for nursing students, assistants, and bedside nursing professionals complete with verbal cues and techniques for confidently and safely assisting patients in the acute care hospital. Over 17 years, I have developed wonderful relationships with nursing colleagues and student interns. Repeated questions over the course of my career include: "How did you get that patient up?... It took three of us to get him/her job... I am floating to this unit and not confident in precautions", and so many more. What is taught in the classroom has been refined by years of experience and I NEVER want a nurse to feel uncomfortable or lack confidence when exercising best practice of mobilizing patients. This guide covers all major patient care groups and associated precautions. The verbal cues and stories included may be humorous, but patients "get it" and this is how I teach my students and nursing colleagues!
