Pushing to the max every step of the way

Emily KavanaughMBA, PT, CCS

Emily Kavanaugh possesses over 17 years of progressive clinical experience across setting, with the majority of her career spent in the acute hospital setting and critical care. She serves as a Clinical Instructor for numerous DPT programs. After 4 years as a team leader of a Level2 Trauma center PT team in Southeast Texas, she obtained a breadth of experience through positions across the country in large academic and community hospitals. In 2010, Emily saw the opportunity to significantly improve patient outcomes and influence ICU program development for the Trauma, Neuro, and Cardiothoracic surgery programs at Mary Washington Hospital in Fredericksburg, VA. In Mat 2014, Emily achieved the CCS designation from the ABPTS and completed her MBA in May 2015. Most recently, Emily has championed the contribution of physical therapy practice as an integral part of the value-based healthcare models that healthcare organization desire to achieve. She established ELK Mobility, LLC May 2016 and actively addresses, educates, and guides hospital executive management teams in understanding operational benefits of skilled therapy investments, then consults to train and provide departmental guidance to bridge gaps in current knowledge and/or practice.

Professional Associations:

  • American Physical Therapy Association (APTA)
  • American Board of Physical Therapy Specialists (ABPTS)
  • American College of Healthcare Executives
  • ICU Critical Care Rehab Conference, Johns Hopkins Medical Center
